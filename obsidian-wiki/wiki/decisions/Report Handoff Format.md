# Report Handoff Format

## Decision

External handoff reports should read as direct professional briefs to the
recipient. Avoid third-person language about the reader and avoid breaking the
fourth wall.

## Report Requirements

- Put the project link front and center.
- Emphasize the operator as the deliverable.
- Use direct executive style.
- Include concrete prompts the recipient can hand to Codex or Claude.

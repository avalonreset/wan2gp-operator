# Wan2GP Operator

Repository: https://github.com/avalonreset/wan2gp-operator

Wan2GP Operator is the local project that lets agents operate WanGP through a
deterministic CLI.

## Current Role

The operator is the handoff artifact for users who want WanGP results without
learning every WanGP setting or ComfyUI-style graph workflow.
